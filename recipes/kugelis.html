Kugelis

Kugelis is one of the favourite potato dishes in Lithuania. With easy recipe and high potential for personalised modifications, you are sure to fall in love with this dish!

Ingredients:

  • 2 large onions, diced

  • 1/2 cup butter

  • 5 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and shredded

  • 4 eggs

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Cook onion over medium heat until caramelised. Remove from head and stir in butter to melt.

  3. Stir shredded potatoes into the onion mixture, then stir in the eggs until well combined. Pour into a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.

  4. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour, or until golden on top. Remove and let stand for 10 minutes before slicing into 3x3-inch squares. Serve hot.

Enjoy!
